About Me

Welcome!

I'm Tianai (Tina) Liu and I'm currently a 3rd year student at the University of British Columbia. I'm in the process of receiving a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science with a minor in Management.

I'm an aspiring Project Manager with an interest in UI/UX and AI. I have experience developing back-end software as well as managing teams. At UBC, I worked as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant and Social Media Coordinator. I also volunteered for the QSCU as the Events Coordinator and the UBCO Tennis Club as the Vice President. In and out of the classroom, I am a hardworking, enthusaistic student that pursues new ideas and initiatives.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing tennis, watching movies (100% recommend The Social Network and Interstellar), traveling (currently on exchange at NUS), and listening to music (feel free to follow me on spotify)!

Feel free to learn more about my professional and personal expriences that have shaped who I am today. Reach out if you have any questions or opportunities.
